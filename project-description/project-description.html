NextReel

A movie discovery platform designed to help users find what to watch next.

Purpose

To help users discover their next movie by offering personalized suggestions across genres and titles, while also providing a space to share and rate their viewing experience.

Scenario

“What are some good new releases I can watch tonight, and where can I stream them?”

“I’m thinking about watching [Movie Title] — what do other people think about it?”

Color Schema and Fonts

Primary: Soft Black (#222831)

Accent: Sunset Orange (#FF5722)

Secondary: Teal (#009688)

Text: Off-White (#F5F5F5), Warm Gray (#9E9E9E)

Fonts:
